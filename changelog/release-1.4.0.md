Title: Game Activity Refresh
Summary: New invites, online indicator, and Minecraft 26.2 support

## More info before you join
Game invites now display more details about what you've been invited to. You can now see the world name or server IP, the icon, and how many players are currently connected, all before you hit join from the invite notification.

## Game invites in your chats
Game invites now live directly in your Essential chats. Never lose track of an invite again. Open the Essential social menu, find your friend, and hit join. No more asking your friends to re-send the invite. A few things worth knowing:
- World invites expire when the world goes offline or you're removed.
- Server invites stay indefinitely, even when the server goes offline.

## Online indicator
See which of your friends are online at a glance. The online indicator now shows next to friends in the Essential social menu.

## Last online timestamp
See when your friends were last online. It now shows when your friends were last online in the friends list in the Essential social menu.
- You can stop sharing this info with friends in the Essential settings.

## Simplified Discord activity status
We've updated what information shows on your Discord profile when you enable activity sharing. We've simplified it to only show relevant game info, not when you are visiting specific menus, like it used to be.

## Minecraft 26.2
We've updated Essential to support Minecraft 26.2 for Fabric.

## Other Improvements
- Added a disabled state to the "Invite to Game" button during cooldown to provide clearer player feedback in the Essential social menu
- Improved error message when gifting a cosmetic that is no longer available
- Updated the help link in the account authentication failed modal to point to the wiki

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed cosmetics displaying incorrectly to others and saving incorrectly on Essential's servers under specific circumstances
- Fixed Discord activity status not working on Windows for Minecraft 26.1
- Fixed edited messages incorrectly increasing unread notification count
