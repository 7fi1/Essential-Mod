Title: Minecraft 26.1 Support
Summary: Minecraft 26.1 support and improvements

## New Version
- Added support for Minecraft 26.1 Fabric

## Improvements
- Improved modals shown when the Essential network is unreachable to provide clear and detailed information on the likely causes
- Added clickable version info in Essential settings for quick copying

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed bug in Forge resulting in logs being spammed with EventSubclassTransformer-related errors on Minecraft 1.16 - 1.21.5
- Fixed the "Darken Minecraft button texture" option not working on Minecraft 1.21.2 - 1.21.5
- Fixed a crash with the "Darken Minecraft button texture" option on some systems on Minecraft 1.21.2 - 1.21.5
- Fixed world invite notifications staying around after invite is revoked
- Fixed crash caused by rapidly enabling and disabling capes on Minecraft 1.21.9 - 1.21.11

## Compatibility
- Updated bundled Mixin to 0.8.7 on Minecraft 1.8.9 and 1.12.2
